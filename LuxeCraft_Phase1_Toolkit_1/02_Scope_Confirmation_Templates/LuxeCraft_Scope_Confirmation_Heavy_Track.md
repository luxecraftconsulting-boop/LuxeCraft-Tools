## What's Included in This Engagement

### Build Phase (Weeks 1–6)

LuxeCraft will perform the following work during the build phase:

- Full construction of cost catalog from current operations
- Full construction of budget templates with formulas, assemblies, and reusable structures
- Full construction of schedule templates with task structures, dependencies, and milestones
- Full build-out of selections catalog including allowances, packages, and customer-facing experience
- Full configuration of user roles, permissions, and team mapping
- Full build-out of customer pipeline stages and lead-to-close logic
- Full document template suite development (contracts, proposals, change orders, invoices, selections)
- Cost inboxing setup and routing configuration
- Full vendor record build-out and organization
- Standard integration configuration (e.g., QuickBooks Online), including data migration where applicable
- Migration support from prior platform (BuilderTrend, CoConstruct, or spreadsheets) for active jobs
- Specifications library construction across primary trade categories
- Account hygiene cleanup and naming standardization
- Customization of dashboards and reports for the Heavy track scope

### Team Rollout Phase (Weeks 7–11)

The Heavy Track team rollout proceeds at a condensed cadence across four tiers, with multiple tools rolled out per week:

- **Tier 1 — Daily Habits (Week 7):** Time tracking, messaging, daily logs, to-dos
- **Tier 2 — Workflow Basics (Weeks 8–9):** Schedules, customer pipeline, documents, files, plans
- **Tier 3 — Client-Facing Tools (Week 10):** Selections, allowances, proposals, contracts
- **Tier 4 — Financial Operations (Week 11):** Budgets, estimates, change orders, purchase orders, invoicing, cost tracking

The Heavy Track condensed cadence requires high engagement from leadership and the Success Champion to maintain pace. Each weekly tool rollout includes leadership pre-approval, a live team kickoff session, a practice week, and an adoption check.

### Success Champion Training (Weekly)

Your designated Success Champion receives a 30–45 minute training session each week, learning each tool one week ahead of the team rollout. Heavy Track Success Champions receive additional context during the build phase to prepare for the condensed rollout cadence. This includes the Success Champion Playbook and 12-chapter weekly reading schedule.

### Optimization & Handoff (Week 12)

- Final adoption review with leadership
- Role-based dashboards and KPI reports finalized
- 60/90-day plan for continued refinement
- Transition into the JobTread Support Lab membership

## Heavy Track-Specific Boundaries

The following items remain **outside the scope of the Heavy Track**. If any of the items below become required, they will be quoted as a separate engagement via the Change Request Process:

- Engagements requiring extension beyond the contracted 13-week timeline (extended engagements quoted separately as a 16-week timeline)
- Multi-entity or multi-company architecture beyond a single primary entity
- Custom software development, API integrations, or third-party tool development beyond JobTread's native capabilities
- Build-out of more than five custom dashboards beyond standard role-based templates
- Ongoing post-engagement build work (this transitions to Support Lab membership)
- Acquisition or due diligence support, business valuation work, or strategic consulting outside JobTread implementation


## Universal Out of Scope

The following items are outside the scope of every LuxeCraft Phase 1 implementation engagement, regardless of track:

- Bookkeeping, accounting reconciliation, or ongoing financial management
- Hiring decisions, HR consulting, or personnel management
- Custom software development, API integrations beyond JobTread's native capabilities, or third-party tool development
- Implementation, configuration, or training on non-JobTread software platforms
- Ongoing subscription costs for JobTread, integrations, or related tools (these remain the client's responsibility)
- Day-to-day operational management of the client's team or projects
- On-site visits or in-person training (all sessions are conducted via Zoom unless otherwise contracted)
- Legal, tax, or compliance advice

## Change Request Process

Any request that falls outside this defined scope will follow this process:

1. **Submit:** The client submits a written change request describing what is needed and why.
2. **Review:** LuxeCraft reviews the request within 3 business days and provides one of three responses:
   - **Accommodate within current scope** — if the work fits the existing engagement without disruption.
   - **Defer to Support Lab** — if the work is better addressed in the post-engagement membership.
   - **Quote a scope addendum** — if the work requires additional time, fees, or a track upgrade.
3. **Approve:** No additional work begins until the client approves the change in writing.

This process protects both parties from scope creep and ensures the original engagement timeline remains achievable.
