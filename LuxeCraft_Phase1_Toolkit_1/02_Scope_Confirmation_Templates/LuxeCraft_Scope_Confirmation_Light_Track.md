## What's Included in This Engagement

### Build Phase (Weeks 1–2)

LuxeCraft will perform the following work during the build phase:

- Refinement of existing cost catalog, including cleanup, organization, and gap fills
- Review and adjustment of existing budget templates
- Verification and refinement of user roles and permissions
- Cleanup of existing customer pipeline stages and naming
- Review of existing document templates with minor adjustments
- Verification of integrations and account hygiene
- Customization of dashboards and reports for the Light track scope

### Team Rollout Phase (Weeks 3–11)

The Light Track team rollout proceeds at a standard cadence across four tiers:

- **Tier 1 — Daily Habits (Weeks 3–4):** Time tracking, messaging, daily logs, to-dos
- **Tier 2 — Workflow Basics (Weeks 5–6):** Schedules, customer pipeline, documents, files, plans
- **Tier 3 — Client-Facing Tools (Weeks 7–8):** Selections, allowances, proposals, contracts
- **Tier 4 — Financial Operations (Weeks 9–10):** Budgets, estimates, change orders, purchase orders, invoicing, cost tracking
- **Tier 4 continued (Week 11):** Buffer week for any catch-up or refinement before optimization phase

Each weekly tool rollout includes leadership pre-approval, a live team kickoff session, a practice week, and an adoption check.

### Success Champion Training (Weekly)

Your designated Success Champion receives a 30–45 minute training session each week, learning each tool one week ahead of the team rollout. This includes the Success Champion Playbook and 12-chapter weekly reading schedule.

### Optimization & Handoff (Week 12)

- Final adoption review with leadership
- Role-based dashboards and KPI reports finalized
- 60/90-day plan for continued refinement
- Transition into the JobTread Support Lab membership

## Light Track-Specific Boundaries

The following items are **outside the scope of the Light Track**. If any of the items below become required during the engagement, a **track upgrade to Moderate or Heavy** will be discussed and quoted via the Change Request Process:

- Building a new cost catalog from scratch (Light track refines what exists)
- Building a new budget template from scratch
- Building a new schedule template from scratch
- Building a new selections catalog from scratch
- Migrating from BuilderTrend, CoConstruct, or another platform with active job data
- Significant team data import or cleanup beyond standard refinement
- Custom dashboard or report development beyond standard role-based templates


## Universal Out of Scope

The following items are outside the scope of every LuxeCraft Phase 1 implementation engagement, regardless of track:

- Bookkeeping, accounting reconciliation, or ongoing financial management
- Hiring decisions, HR consulting, or personnel management
- Custom software development, API integrations beyond JobTread's native capabilities, or third-party tool development
- Implementation, configuration, or training on non-JobTread software platforms
- Ongoing subscription costs for JobTread, integrations, or related tools (these remain the client's responsibility)
- Day-to-day operational management of the client's team or projects
- On-site visits or in-person training (all sessions are conducted via Zoom unless otherwise contracted)
- Legal, tax, or compliance advice

## Change Request Process

Any request that falls outside this defined scope will follow this process:

1. **Submit:** The client submits a written change request describing what is needed and why.
2. **Review:** LuxeCraft reviews the request within 3 business days and provides one of three responses:
   - **Accommodate within current scope** — if the work fits the existing engagement without disruption.
   - **Defer to Support Lab** — if the work is better addressed in the post-engagement membership.
   - **Quote a scope addendum** — if the work requires additional time, fees, or a track upgrade.
3. **Approve:** No additional work begins until the client approves the change in writing.

This process protects both parties from scope creep and ensures the original engagement timeline remains achievable.
